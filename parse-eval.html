Parse-resume eval harness

Issue #241 · dev-only · WebGPU required · not bundled into production.

Runs BOTH the SEPARATE pass (parseResumeWithLlm + critiqueResumeWithLlm) AND the COMBINED pass (analyzeResumeWithLlm) over 3 inline PII-safe resume fixtures (software-engineer, marketing-coordinator, recent-grad) and scores field-level accuracy. Two reports come out: the original parse-only one (PR #241) and a side-by-side comparison (PR #262, load-bearing quality gate).

One model per tab on purpose: cycling multi-GB models in a single tab is fragile on consumer GPUs. Run each model in a fresh tab, download its report, then open a new tab for the next model.

Idle. Click “Run eval” to start.
